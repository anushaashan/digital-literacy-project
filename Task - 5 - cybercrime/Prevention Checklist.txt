Stay Safe Online – Prevention Checklist


1. Never share your UPI PIN or OTP with anyone, even if they claim to be from your bank or apps like Google Pay or PhonePe.
2. Do not approve unknown “Collect” requests on UPI—receiving money never requires entering a PIN.
3. Verify links before clicking—avoid suspicious messages, emails, or fake websites.
4. Enable two-factor authentication (2FA) on all important accounts (email, banking, social media).
5. Use strong, unique passwords and avoid using the same password everywhere.
6. Do not share personal details (Aadhaar, bank info, passwords) on calls or messages.
7. Avoid public Wi-Fi for financial transactions, especially in cafes or malls.
8. Regularly update apps and phone software to fix security vulnerabilities.
9. Install apps only from trusted sources like Play Store/App Store—avoid APK files.
10. Immediately report fraud on the National Cyber Crime Portal (cybercrime.gov.in) or call Helpline 1930 for quick action.
