SOCIAL MEDIA DO’S AND DON’TS
 Do’s:
1. Update your apps regularly to stay protected from security threats
2. Use two-factor authentication (2FA) for extra account safety
3. Follow trustworthy and informative accounts
4. Report fake accounts or harmful content
5. Take breaks from social media to maintain mental well-being
________________


Don’ts:
1. Do not accept friend requests from strangers
2. Do not overshare your daily location or live updates
3. Do not spread rumors or unverified news
4. Do not use public Wi-Fi for logging into important accounts
5. Do not compare your life with others online excessively
